Preserving the Machine

Preventative maintenance is the key to longevity. Heat is the primary enemy of silicon, and dust is the primary cause of heat.

The Technician's Toolkit

Everyone should have these essentials in their lab kit:

  • Anti-Static Wrist Strap: To prevent ESD damage.
  • Isopropyl Alcohol (99%): For cleaning thermal paste.
  • Magnetic Screwdriver Set: For precision handling.
  • Compressed Air: For non-contact cleaning.
