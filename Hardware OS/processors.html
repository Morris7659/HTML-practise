Gallery of Silicon

Visualizing the jump from DIP(Dual In-line Package) to modern LGA(Land Grid Array), in simple terms, moving from long pins on the sides of a chip (DIP) to flat pads on the bottom of a chip (LGA), allowing for much smaller parts and thousands of more connections.
