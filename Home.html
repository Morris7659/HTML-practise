RUAI WARD(No. 1400)

Ruai ward is an electoral area in Nairobi County within Kasarani Constituency.

It is part of Njiru Sub-County covering an area or abot 98 square kilometers.

It includes areas such as Ruai and Kamulu, bodering Njiru Ward.

Key Elements That Reflect Ruai Ward

Ruai Ward can be described as a rapidly expanding "frontier" of Nairobi with elements such as:

  1. The "Eastlands Hub" Strategy
  2. Urban Infrastructure Anchor
  3. Community and Recreation

Ruai Ward is also centered on its strategic role as an infrustructural anchor for Nairobi with major landmarks including:

  • Dandora Estate Sewage
  • Ruai Market
  • St. Cecilia Catholic Church
